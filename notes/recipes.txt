Making classic breakfast pancakes requires mixing basic pantry staples together. In a large bowl, whisk together one cup of all-purpose flour, two tablespoons of sugar, a pinch of salt, one cup of milk, one egg, and two tablespoons of melted butter.

For a perfect Italian tomato sauce, start by gently sautéing minced garlic and chopped onions in olive oil until they are soft and translucent. Add crushed San Marzano tomatoes, a sprig of fresh basil, and simmer on low heat for thirty minutes to let the flavors deepen.

Brewing a great cup of coffee at home depends heavily on the water temperature and the grind size of the beans. For a standard pour-over method, the water should be kept between 90°C and 96°C to extract the optimal flavor without burning the grinds.
